Best Kanye album, the amount of features on all the lights is insane, the sample of Avril 14th used on Blame Game is great although he initially refused to credit Aphex Twin for it. Runaway is and amazing song, I like the synth on his voice at the end. The Bon Iver vocals on lost in the world are amazing, as well as the transition from that into Who Will Survive In America. I initally was not a fan of Power because i'd heard it a lot beforehand but now i've grown to like it and the King Crimson sample is fun. Jay-Z's verse on Monster is kinda eh at first but is kinda funny after a while. Dark Fantasy is great as an opening and Gorgeous is a good follow up. Best song is Runaway, worst song is So Appalled although they're both 10's.

Aphex Twin has lots of good music but I feel like this is his best album apart from maybe Drukqs which features his most popular song, Avril 14th. I also like Flim off his other album "Come To Daddy", another honourable mention is the single "Windowlicker" but now onto this album, its his first and a collection of music he made in the years 1985 to 1992 as the title suggests, he was not going to release any of it but was convinced to by others. My favourite song from the album is Xtal, it feels really wet and windy which is oddly quite comforting, the 5th track "I" is also really good although a bit too short in my opinion, Heliosphan is also quite good an much more energetic than lots of the others, although still really wet sounding. The Willy Wonka sample in "We Are The Music Makers" is funny and the entire album is quite impressive considering at this point Richard D. James, the man behind Aphex Twin had no musical training.
Lots of synth done really well and mixed really well with more acoustic sounds, giving lots of the music really homemade feeling. The vocals on Home, and Dance Yrself Clean are really pleasing to me with Dance Yrself Clean being his most popular song, the live versions of these songs are also really good.
